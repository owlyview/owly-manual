00 · Introduction

Welcome to Owly View — Your Baby's Guardian Owl

Owly View is a free AI baby monitor app that works with just two smartphones.

📷
Hub Phone
Camera near baby
transmits video
👁️
Viewer Phone
Parent's phone
receives video
📋 What you need
  • 2 Android smartphones (different models OK)
  • Both phones on the same Wi-Fi
  • Search "Owly View" on Google Play and install
1
📡 Real-time Video Monitoring
Hub phone's camera streams live to the viewer phone over Wi-Fi via direct P2P connection — no external server.
2
😢 AI Cry Detection
AI analyzes audio in real-time and sends an alert to the viewer phone when crying is detected. All processing stays on-device.
3
👶 AI Face Detection
Alerts you when the baby's face disappears from the camera — whether flipped over or out of frame.
4
🦉 Baby Care Box
Manage feeding, sleep, medicine reminders, and growth records all in one place.
5
🔒 Completely Free & Private
All features are free. Video and audio never pass through an external server, keeping your privacy protected.

🧠 AI Smart Detection
Toggle cry detection and sleep safety (face) detection on/off separately. Cry detection has a dB threshold slider; sleep detection has a delay slider (seconds before alerting when face is lost). Turning the switch off means you won't receive that alert.
If alerts fire too often, raise the dB threshold or increase the delay
🌡️ Env Sensor Thresholds
Only available when a Bluetooth temperature/humidity sensor is connected. Set acceptable ranges for room temperature and humidity — alerts fire when the values go out of range. The panel appears blurred without a sensor.
🔔 Device Alerts & Sound
Turn on Background Alerts to receive cry and face alerts even when the viewer phone screen is off. The alert volume slider controls how loud alert sounds are.
🔒 Security & Connections
Copy or share the access code invite link with family. In the family list, toggle each member's alert notifications on/off, and edit their nickname (display name) directly.
Pending family entry requests can also be approved or declined here
A red dot on the Settings tab icon means there's a pending request
